Frequently Asked Questions

Why do you require a consultation before booking?

We want to ensure we are a good fit for your pet's needs. The consultation gives everyone a chance to get their questions answered and discuss the finer details of the sit so you fabulous homeowners are satisfied, and so we know our way around and don't need to bother you unnecessarily during your time away!

What's included in your house-sitting service?

We provide feeding, walks, companionship, and whatever else your doggo(s) require(s), as well as a move-out clean you'd swear was done by professional cleaners.

How long can you stay at our home?

We have experience doing sits from a single night up to 3 weeks long! However long you need us is what we can do.

What happens in case of a pet emergency?

We are trained to handle emergencies and will contact you immediately. Where to take your dog and who else you might want us to call are details we go over during the walkthrough/consultation.

How do you handle updates during our absence?

We provide regular updates and photos of your pet, however frequently you want them!
